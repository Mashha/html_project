Tracking

Go back

Tracking is like the "blind search" . In this fun activity dog also uses his senses to complete the exercise, but he has to do it in a prescribed way.

Equipment you will need, besides a big grassy field:

  • Harness
  • 10m long line
  • Treats
  • A few articles

He is not looking for a specific odor like in scentwork, but rather a footprint scent and soil damage that is left in the grass after we walked on it.

You can make several straight lines with corners and articles in between. It might look pretty boring if you are not enthusiastic about it, but for the dog it's fun.
