Obedience was my passion to teach. Something so complicated but so nice at the end.

Teaching obedience can be done with positive reinforcement and not much equipment is needed for it.

Obedience is something you can do just for fun or more seriously, like competing.

The obedience scheme is built from a few different exercises connected in one long pattern.

  • Heel on a leash
  • Heel off-leash
  • Change pace in heel
  • Sit-stay
  • Down-stay
  • Recall
  • Heel in a group
  • Long down in the distance
