I started teaching my dogs tricks to make them think and fulfill their needs for mental exerciese. Plus because it is fun.

When teaching a trick, you should be aware of how the dog learns and if he is understanding it or not.

Every trick should be divided into several smaller steps, so it is easier for the dog to understand and achieve the goal. Sessions should be short and effective.

Tricks can be simple, for example:

  • Give me a paw
  • Down
  • Spin
  • Roll-over
  • Bark
  • Wave
  • Focus

Or more difficult or complex ones, like:

  • Put away toys
  • Crawling
  • Walking backwards
  • Stack rings on a stick
  • Roll up in a blanket
  • Limp
